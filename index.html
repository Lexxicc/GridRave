HOW TO PLAY

THE GRID

A 4×4 grid of pads lights up in time to the beat. Tap each lit pad in time to score.

RUN A SONG

Each game plays one full track — INTRO, VERSE, BUILD, DROP, BREAK, BUILD, DROP, OUTRO. The game ends when the song ends. No lives — just play through.

DANGER PADS

Red glowing pads are traps. Do NOT tap them. Tap a danger pad and your combo resets. Let them expire on their own — nothing happens.

MISSED BEATS

Miss a lit pad and it just fades. No life penalty, but your combo breaks and the miss is added to your tally for the run.

COMBO MULTIPLIER

Hit pads consecutively to build your multiplier (×1 → ×5). Miss a pad or tap danger and it resets to ×1.

SCORING

Each hit = 10 pts × your current multiplier. Max combo hit = 50 pts. Final accuracy = hits / (hits + missed).

SECTIONS

Pad density tracks the song. Sparse in the intro and outro, building through verses, peak in the drops, breathing room in the breakdown.
