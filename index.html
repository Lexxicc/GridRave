HOW TO PLAY

THE GRID

A 4×4 grid of pads lights up in time to the beat. Tap lit pads before they expire to score. Miss a lit pad — lose a life.

DANGER PADS

Red glowing pads are traps. Do NOT tap them. Tap a danger pad and you lose a life. Let them expire on their own — nothing happens.

COMBO MULTIPLIER

Hit pads consecutively to build your multiplier (×1 → ×5). Miss or tap danger and it resets to ×1.

SCORING

Each hit = 10 pts × your current multiplier. Max combo hit = 50 pts.

LEVELS

Every 10 hits the BPM rises and pads expire faster. There is no ceiling — survive as long as possible.

⚡ BEAT DROP

Every 16 bars the Beat Drop fires for 4 seconds — locked to the BPM. No life penalty during the drop — tap everything and score freely.

LIVES

3 lives. All gone — game over.
